INTRODUCTION

Intangible Cultural Heritage (ICH), as defined by UNESCO, encompasses practices, expressions, and skills that form the nonphysical intellectual wealth of a community. This includes folklore, customs, beliefs, and traditions, distinct from tangible heritage like buildings and monuments.

Join us in exploring the richness of China's intangible cultural heritage and in carrying forward our cultural legacy!
