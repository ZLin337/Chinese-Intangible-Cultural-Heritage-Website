Reflecting

I developed this website in order to publicize my country's intangible cultural heritage to people in other countries. I am well aware that my country has a long history of 5,000 years, and this rich cultural heritage is the treasure of our nation. Therefore, through this website, I hope to show the world the unique cultural charms of our nation, so that more people can understand and appreciate our excellent culture.

In the process of making this website, I learned many skills about website design and development. I learned how to design a user-friendly interface, make the website easy to navigate and browse, and mastered the techniques of website production, including knowledge of HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

During the design process it was found that fixed values should be used sparingly when writing css files, instead using percentages etc. to ensure that the website automatically adjusts and can render well on a variety of devices. This has provided me with good lessons for developing websites in the future.

However, I also realized an important issue during the process: a good website should not only have rich content, but also a beautiful interface. Designing an aesthetically pleasing interface requires a certain amount of artistry and aesthetic vision. If I had realized this earlier, I might have spent more time looking for background images and so on that better fit the theme and make the site look more aesthetically pleasing. This made me realize that the balance between content and aesthetics is crucial in the website design process. Only when both are present can users be truly engaged and impressed.

Overall, through the process of actually designing a website, I learned a lot about important principles and techniques of website design and realized that continuous learning and improvement is the key to designing great websites. By creating this website, I have not only gained technical growth, but also deepened my passion for cultural preservation and heritage. I hope that through this website, I can connect people from different countries and regions, and more people can recognize and understand China's intangible cultural heritage.
