1.1 Introduce Your Topic/Passion

Chinese Intangible Cultural Heritage Website

As a Chinese international student from a country with a history spanning five millennia, I have always been captivated by the rich tapestry of our country's cultural heritage. It is a legacy that has endured the test of time, passed down through centuries of change. Motivated by my love for our cultural treasures and a sense of urgency spurred by their gradual disappearance, I created a platform dedicated to promoting China's intangible cultural heritage. Immersing myself in folk literature, music and dance, traditional drama and storytelling, acrobatics and sports, fine arts, and traditional medicine, I've curated a comprehensive showcase of these cultural elements on Chinese Intangible Cultural Heritage Website. Whether it's delving into ancient folklore, experiencing the charm of traditional music, or witnessing the magic of Chinese theater, my aim is to educate, engage, and ignite a love for intangible cultural heritage. I work hard to promote China's excellent intangible cultural heritage on my website, hoping to attract more people's attention to this excellent culture and attract more people to understand, appreciate and inherit it. I believe that through collective efforts we can ensure that these valuable cultural traditions are protected and continue to grow. Join us in exploring the richness of China's intangible cultural heritage and in carrying forward our cultural legacy!

2.3 & 2.4 Storyboard + Video / Javascript

Javascript file feedback.js main js gallery js explore index js

I use Javascript to achieve the following functions:

1. Display the current date and time: After the DOM loads, the script gets the container on the page that displays the current date and time. Get the current Date and time through the 'date' object and format it in a localized string format. The formatted date and time are then displayed in the container.

2.Handle email subscriptions: The script listens for subscription form submission events. When the form is submitted, the script validates the format of the E-mail input. If the format is invalid, the form submission is blocked and an error message is displayed. If the format is valid, the script sends a POST request to the '/subscribe' endpoint through the Fetch API, sending a subscription request that includes an email. Depending on the result of the request, the script displays the corresponding subscription success or failure message and handles possible network errors.

3. Slider system: The script switches the slide between two content blocks by listening for the click event of the left and right scroll buttons. It adjusts the current slider index and updates the 'transform' style of the content container based on the index to slide it to the desired position.

4. Initialize the gallery and switch images automatically: In each gallery section, the script sets a timer to switch to the next image every 3 seconds. It hides the currently displayed image, increases the index to the next image, and displays the next image (by removing the hidden class), making the image loop. This implements a circular slide show within the gallery section.

5. Provide validation for form submissions: Verify fields such as name, email address, gender, feedback, etc

6. Video playback control function: Play, pause, mute, or cancel a video
